LOCAL SOFTWARE ENGINEER EXPLORES POTENTIAL JOBS

In a (not overly) surprising turn of events, a local software engineering student has redirected his job hunt from tech companies to McDonald’s after over 300 unsuccessful applications. “I don’t know how many people will take my ‘Meat Rotation Technician’ role seriously, but it cannot be worse than putting a ‘Hello World’ project on my portfolio,” said the student, wishing to remain anonymous after putting such a misleading title on his LinkedIn.

Many other Software students echo similar thoughts, with another one recollecting this time as “terrible and low quality”. At the end of the day, our local Software Engineer is already doing better, given that he at least has a job, unlike many of his peers.
